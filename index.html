Getting started:

  • Understanding security well enough and being able to shift it left into your development team without adverse side effects is hard.
  • “Holistic Info-Sec for Web Developers - Fascicle 0” teaches you everything you need to know to build a security focus and knowledge into your development team(s). The book series is useful as both a tutorial and reference book.
  • The other parts of the book series build on the foundation established in Fascicle 0

Holistic Info-Sec for Web Developers - Fascicle 0

Holistic and in-depth coverage of:

  • The chosen threat modelling approach - application to the development team
  • Setting up your tool-belt
  • The process of penetration testing - application to the development team
  • A collection of processes and practises formulated from penetration testing, useful for augmenting each and every Scrum Sprint
